Limaon

Let P be a point and C be a circle whose center is not P. Then the envelope of those circles whose center lies on C and that pass through P is a limaon.

def myCircle(z,r):
    N  = 50
    dt = 1.0/N
    t = np.arange(0,1+dt,dt)
    return z + r*np.exp(2j*np.pi*t)
